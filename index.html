Claude Accord

General Info

Claude Accord is a framework for genuine human-AI collaboration — not a prompt library, not a chatbot, not a productivity tool. A protocol for those who want to understand what they're actually talking to.

01

Choose your context

Personal, Business, or Creative — each tier gives you a dedicated space calibrated for what you actually need.

02

Install the protocol

A framework that works whether you spend 5 minutes or 5 hours with it. Surface or depth — your choice.

03

It matures with you

The protocol grows as your understanding grows. Updates aren't patches — they're a record of what we've learned.

Claude Accord Creative

For those who make things

AI can generate. That's not the problem. The problem is that generated content has no voice, no weight, no risk. This is for creators who refuse to outsource their thinking.

01

Collaboration, not replacement

Your voice stays yours. AI brings what you don't have — not a substitute for what you do.

02

Depth over speed

The protocol is slow enough to be useful. What takes a minute to generate takes hours to think through — and that's the point.

03

Risk still required

The framework doesn't remove the hard part. It gives you better company while you do it.
